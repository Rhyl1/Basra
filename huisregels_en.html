House Rules

  • Consumption is mandatory.
  • Under 18 years of age, no access. In case of doubt, proof of identity is required.

  • Aggression will not be tolerated, in case of violation immediate removal will follow.
  • Trade in stolen goods is strictly forbidden.
  • It is forbidden to call and/or hang out in front of the door; think of our neighbours.
  • It is forbidden to smoke tobacco products inside.
